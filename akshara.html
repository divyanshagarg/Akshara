We are a nonprofit organization (NPO), an organization dedicated to a social cause. To organise a camp visit to your village or to donate your service or land to us please contact us through various social media handles. Please step forward open heartedly for this noble cause.




FUND RAISED


Past year we raised a fund of 2 crore. Enough to help existing centers/kids associated with food, books, clothes. Though this fund was not enough to establish new centers and reach out to more kids.

We therefore request you to support us in large numbers in whichever way it's possible either by donating clothes, books, money or any other way such as rendering your valuable service and time as a volunteer etc.



OUR SUPPORT


Past year our support chain icreased by 2716 members. Who supported us by any way they could.

We request you to lend us your support and together we'll bring a smile on every face possible.



REACHED TO


Past year we succeeded to help 4000 kids by providing them healthcare facilities, quality education and nutritious food. We are motivated when these kids smile, we are motivated when these kids don't sleep hungry, we are motivated when these kids recite poem and not abusive language.

We request you to help needy kids all around you wherever you see them may it be on a roadside or may it be your maid's kid. Set an example that others can follow :)





ABOUT US



Akshara - name of goddess "SARASWATI" - Hindu goddess of knowledge, music, art, wisdom, and learning.
We at akshara are dedicated wholly towards child's overall development. We visit villages,slums etc.
We provide free education to needy kids. Not only education we provide nutritious food, basic healthcare to these kids.



If the poor guy can't come to education, education must come to him. - Swami Vivekanand


Going by this quote by swami vivekanand we provide poor, needy kids by a very basic and often ignored requirement "EDUCATION".
Hoping that one day this would change their present circumstances and would make them independent, prosperous being in future.
We have touched many lives since 5 years . We have our 57 centers across many states and 478 full time dedicated volunteers.
